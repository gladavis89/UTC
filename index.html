Mission

UTC - a 501c3 organization

To unite all mediums of the arts together creating communities with a strong artistic and cultural base.

We support and enourage artistic growth unique to each community through advocacy, education of the arts, and commuity services.


"So powerful the light of unity, that it can illuminate the whole world"


Unity Through Community is an Arizona based non-profit with the goal of bringing people together through the arts.

We believe in bridging gaps in the communty and sharing the best of yourself with others.


Our goal is to create spaces throughout the world where like-minded individuals and families can inspire, create, teach, connect, share, and learn from one another.
